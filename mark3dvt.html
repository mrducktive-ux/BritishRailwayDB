About this stock

The Mark 3 Driving Van Trailer (DVT) is a control car operated by Regional on Semi-fast services and by Intercity. It is designed to run at up to 125 mph.

Semi-fastIntercity

Database notes

  • It costs 750 coins and requires a locomotive.
  • It can be used with Class 47, Class 86, and Class 90.
  • The unit has 4 normal liveries and 2 special liveries according to the article text.
  • Its full 125 mph design speed is not yet reachable with current locomotives per the wiki.

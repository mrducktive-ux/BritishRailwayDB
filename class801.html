About the train

The Class 801 is an electric A-Train used on Intercity services. It is one of the 80X trains unlocked through the 80X family.

Intercity

Service badges are now colour-coded to match the service type style, so Intercity, Regional, Semi-fast, and Shuttle stand out more clearly.

Database notes

  • It unlocks with the 80X family purchase.
  • It can multiple-work within class and with Classes 800, 802, 803 and 805.
  • It uses 25 kV AC overhead power.
  • One livery is listed on the official wiki.

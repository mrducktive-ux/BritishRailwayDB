About the train

The Class 180 belongs to the Coradia 1000 family and works in multiple within its own class. It is one of the fastest diesel multiple units available in the game.

Its five-car formation includes First Class, Standard Class, and buffet accommodation.

Database notes

  • Operated by Regional on Semi-fast services.
  • Also operated by Intercity.

About the train

The Class 142 Pacer is a two-car diesel multiple unit operated by Regional. It is one of the available starter trains and can also be purchased separately.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • It can be selected as a free starter train.
  • It can multiple-work with several Sprinter and Turbostar classes.
  • It was remeshed in Version 1.4.0.
  • Eighteen normal liveries and one special livery are listed.

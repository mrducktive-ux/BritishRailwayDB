About the train

The Class 90 is an electric locomotive operated by Regional on Semi-fast services and by Intercity. It hauls Mark 2 or Mark 3 coaches and can work with compatible control cars.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • It reaches a top speed of 110 mph.
  • It has five liveries.
  • Semi-fast formations use four to eight coaches.
  • Intercity formations can use up to ten coaches.

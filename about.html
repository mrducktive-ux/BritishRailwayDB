About us

A fan-built database for British Railway players.

British Railway DB brings rolling stock, routes, weekly items, prices, specifications and unlock information together in one clean place. It is designed to be simple to use on desktop, mobile and controllers.

What British Railway DB is

This is an independent, unofficial reference website made for players of the Roblox game British Railway. It is built to make information quicker to find than searching through lots of separate pages.

Where information comes from

Database details are based primarily on the official British Railway Wiki and information visible in the game. Pages may be updated whenever official information changes.

What the project covers

  • Active rolling stock and technical specifications
  • Route packs, requirements and service colours
  • Weekly Shop items and recent history
  • Future player tools and controller-friendly features

Accuracy and corrections

Mistakes can happen, especially after game updates. Corrections should include the affected page and an official source so changes can be checked before the database is updated.

Privacy

The current version is a static website. It does not contain account sign-ins, tracking systems or a player-data database.

Important disclaimer

British Railway DB is not affiliated with British Railway, Fourth Rail, Roblox or any real-world railway company. Names and game information belong to their respective owners.

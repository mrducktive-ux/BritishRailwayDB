About the train

The Class 153 is a single-car diesel-hydraulic multiple unit used on Regional and Shuttle services. It is the shortest public train listed in the game.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • Each unit contains one coach.
  • It can be coupled into two- or three-car formations.
  • It works with several other Sprinter and Turbostar classes.
  • Six liveries are listed.

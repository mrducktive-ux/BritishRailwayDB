About the train

The Class 86 is an electric locomotive operated on Semi-fast and Intercity services. It can haul Mark 2 or Mark 3 coaches with a compatible driving trailer or another electric locomotive.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • The Class 86/1 reaches 110 mph.
  • The Class 86/2 and Class 86/4 reach 100 mph.
  • It has six normal liveries and three special Heritage liveries.
  • It can work with the Class 90 and compatible coaching stock.

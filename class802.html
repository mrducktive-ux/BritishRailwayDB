About the train

The Class 802 is a bi-mode A-Train operated on Semi-fast and Intercity services and is part of the 80X family.

Semi-fastIntercity

Service badges are now colour-coded to match the service type style, so Intercity, Regional, Semi-fast, and Shuttle stand out more clearly.

Database notes

  • It unlocks with the 80X family purchase.
  • It can multiple-work within class and with Classes 800, 801, 803 and 805.
  • It uses 25 kV AC overhead power as well as diesel power.
  • Six liveries are listed, with five public according to the wiki.

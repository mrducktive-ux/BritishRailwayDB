The Mark 2 Driving Brake Standard Open (DBSO) is a passenger control car operated by Regional and Intercity. It is used with compatible locomotive-hauled Mark 2 sets.

Database notes

  • It costs 750 coins and requires the Mark 2 coaches first.
  • It can be used with Class 47, Class 86, and Class 90.
  • The unit has 4 normal liveries and 1 special livery according to the article text.

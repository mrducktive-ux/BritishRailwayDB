About the train

The Class 230 is a converted D-Train multiple unit used on Regional and Shuttle services. It can be selected as a starter train or bought separately.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • It can be chosen as a free starter train.
  • It can also be purchased for 500 coins or via the Starter Pack.
  • Diesel and battery-powered variants are listed on the official wiki.
  • Five liveries are listed.

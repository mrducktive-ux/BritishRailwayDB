About this stock

Mark 3 coaches are passenger carriages operated by Regional and Intercity. They run in 4 to 10 coach formations hauled by compatible locomotives.

RegionalSemi-fastIntercity

Database notes

  • They are free after purchasing any locomotive.
  • They can be hauled by Class 47, Class 86, and Class 90.
  • The article text says there are 5 liveries and 1 special livery.
  • They are designed for a 125 mph top speed.

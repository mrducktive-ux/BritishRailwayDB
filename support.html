Share the database

Tell other British Railway players about the project. More testers means broken links, outdated information and display problems are found faster.

Report incorrect information

Include the page name, what is wrong, the correct information and a link to an official British Railway source whenever possible.

Suggest a feature

Useful ideas include better search tools, comparison pages, controller navigation, station pages and ways to organise weekly items.

Help test the site

Try every menu, page button, image and search box. Reporting exactly which page broke and what device was used makes fixes much easier.

Send verified sources

Official wiki links, in-game screenshots and update information are much more useful than guesses or unsourced community claims.

Verify a supporter account

Enter a Roblox account username to check which BRDB support passes it currently owns.

The checker reads public Roblox ownership information through the BRDB Cloudflare Worker. No Roblox password, cookie or login is requested.

This confirms that the entered account owns a pass. It does not prove that the person entering the username controls that Roblox account.

About the train

The Class 350 is a four-car Desiro electric multiple unit that can operate Regional, Semi-fast and Intercity services.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • Most variants reach 110 mph; the Central Trains / Silverlink version is 100 mph.
  • It uses 25 kV AC overhead power, with 750 V DC third rail listed for the 350/1.
  • Five liveries are listed.
  • It multiple-works within class.

About the train

The Class 47 is a diesel-electric locomotive used by Regional and Intercity. It can haul Mark 2 or Mark 3 coaching stock and can be paired with compatible control cars.

This page is part of the unofficial British Railway DB project and links back to the official wiki for further information.

Database notes

  • The Class 47/4 and Class 47/8 variants are limited to 95 mph.
  • The Class 47/7 variant can reach 100 mph.
  • Consists can use between four and ten coaches depending on service type.
  • Five standard liveries and one special livery are listed.
